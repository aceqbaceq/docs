у их карт быает две вида прошивки 

  IR 
  IT


IR это рейд контроллер верися
IT это hba версия


карты можно шить в обе стороны.
однако  не всяская утилита прошивки это позволяет!


sas2flash/sas3flash

    sas2flash for LSI SAS 6 Gb/sec HBAs (Windows/Linux/EFI)
        Cannot be used to flash to older firmware
        Cannot be used to flash from IT to IR firmware or from IR to IT firmware
        Can be used to flash the controller BIOS and/or EFI
    sas2flsh for LSI SAS 6 Gb/sec HBAs (DOS)
        Can be used to flash to older firmware
        Can be used to flash from IT to IR firmware or from IR to IT firmware
    sas3flash for LSI SAS 12 Gb/sec HBAs (Windows/Linux/EFI)
        Cannot be used to flash to older firmware
        Cannot be used to flash from IT to IR firmware or from IR to IT firmware
        Can be used to flash the controller BIOS and/or EFI
    sas3flsh for LSI SAS 12 Gb/sec HBAs (DOS)
        Can be used to flash to older firmware
        Can be used to flash from IT to IR firmware or from IR to IT firmware
